# Strata — Workforce Intelligence

AI-powered workforce narrative generator for HR leaders and people analytics teams.

Paste your workforce data. Get a board-ready intelligence briefing — not a dashboard, not charts. A structured narrative your leadership can actually act on.

## What it generates

- **The Real Story** — the single most critical signal in your data, stated plainly
- **Workforce Snapshot** — what your organisation actually looks like right now
- **Risk Signals** — what the data is quietly saying before it becomes a problem
- **Momentum Indicators** — what is working and what is stabilising
- **Forward Look** — what leadership should expect in the next 6 months
- **Strategic Playbook** — 3 recommendations with what, why, and a first step

## Built with

- React (create-react-app)
- Framer Motion
- Claude claude-sonnet-4-6 (Anthropic)
- Deployed on Vercel
